ABOUT WHO WE ARE
Based on collective work and shared knowledge, Nuuk aims to favor dialogue and debate, to transform individual knowledge into increased creative potential. Our studio is a architecture practice based in NY and Paris. Today, it includes 150 architects, urban planners, landscape and interior designers of 25 different nationalities. The company principle of Architecture-Studio is the collective conception.
